Competitive Programming

Competitive programming is a mind sport to develop a programmer's logical ability to write fast and efficient code for a given problem.

"Make it work, make it right, make it fast."

-Kent Beck

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) where machines try to achieve human-level cognition without being programmed explicitly to do so.

"What AI and ML lets you do is find the needle in the haystack."

-Bob work

Web Development

Web development is the building and maintenance of websites; it's the work that happens behind the scenes to make a website look great, work fast and perform well with a seamless user experience. This includes everything from single plain-text webpages up to complex web applications.

"Website without visitors is like a ship lost in the horizon."

-Dr. Christopher Dayadag

App Development

Mobile application development is the set of processes and procedures involved in writing software for small, wireless computing devices. Like Web application development, mobile application development has its roots in more traditional software development.

"Android was intended to be very customizable and we welcome innovations."

-Sundar Pichai

AR-VR Development

Augmented reality and virtual reality refer to computer-generated simulations that integrate the real world (AR) or are entirely self-contained (VR). AR applications let you move around in the real world. With VR, you have to remain in the same location because you cannot see your surroundings.

"AR-VR lets you do things virtually which you could never have done in reality."

-Nicolas

Designing

Design is the art or profession of visual communication that combines images, words, and ideas to convey information to an audience, especially to produce a specific effect. Graphic design is communication design; It's a way of conveying ideas through visuals and design.

"Design is not about how it looks & feels but about how it works."

-Steve Jobs

IoT and ROBOTICS

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a system of interrelated, internet-connected network-enabled devices that can collect and transfer data over a wireless network without human intervention. Robotics, design, construction, and use of machines (robots) to perform tasks done traditionally by human beings

"Internet of things will augment your brain."

-Eric Schmidt

CYBERSECURITY

Cybersecurity is the application of technologies, processes, and controls to protect systems, networks, programs, devices, and data from unauthorized access or attack. It is the practice of ensuring confidentiality, integrity, availability of information, and unauthorized exploitation of systems and networks

"A single vulnerability is all an attacker needs."

-Window Snyder

GAME DEVELOPMENT

Game Development is the art of creating games and describes the design, development, and release of a game. It may involve concept generation, design, build, test and release. The key points to keep in mind while game development is about the game mechanics, rewards, player engagement, and level design.

"Reality is broken. Game developers can fix it."

-Jane McGonigal
