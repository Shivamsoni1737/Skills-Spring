Open Idea Challenge

Brainstorm with your friends, make a team of 2-4 and present your game-changing ideas to the esteemed judges, if your team makes it to the top 15, you will be provided with a buffer of 3 days to work on your idea and fabricate a prototype (app or website). Perks: The top 3 rankers will be prized with a nice amount and a free T-shirt.

Coding Contest

The coding contest is an exhilarating time-based competition comprising 5 questions for 3 hours. We have two tracks, one is open for all and the second one is a junior special track that is only for first-year students. Showcase your skills and make it to the top of the herd to grab some sweet prizes, the top three winners will get prize money and free T-shirts !!!

Web Designing

Web designing, a competition for creative minds. Draft an outline for the web page in any tool of your convenience for the specific content provided to you. And guess what would be the prizes, the top three winners will get prize money and free T-shirts !!!!
